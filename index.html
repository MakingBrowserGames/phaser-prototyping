Doggies!

  • High Concept: The night is dark and full of terrors, so tame the beast before they attack.
  • Player Experience Goals: This is a fun-loving game that should bring a warm feeling to your heart at least until night. At night the world changes and the your cute pets become your evil nightmares.
  • Genre: This game is a platformer with some horror elements.
  • Game play: The objective of this game is to collect bones to feed to the different dogs around the world. It takes three bones to tame a dog and prevent it from attacking at night. The world will shift from day to night changing how the dogs behave. At night the dogs turn vicious and deal damage to you so avoid them at all costs. The days only lasts a minute so grab the bone and tame the dogs. To play this game use the arrow keys to move around and to climb ladders. You can not jump so use the ladders effectively.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is exploring the use of inconsistent AI and constantly changing world. This is meant to create a sense of panic in the player as the attempt to collect during the day and survive at night.
  • Features: The change in the world from day to night is an important and unique aspect of the game that changes how players play and how AI react.

  • Story: You are a lost boy at the park trying to play with the doggies, but before you know it the sun is setting and the dogs seem to be getting more frightening.
  • Target Audience: The target audience of this game is primarily fans of the platform genre. This game is designed to appeal to many different people, as well, including fans of 8 or 16 bit games, fans of classic console games and fans of Doggies!
