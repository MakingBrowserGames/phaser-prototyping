The Road

Controls: Use the arrow keys to move around and approach civilians to collect their brains.
  • High Concept: Kill the people to collect brains and get a high-score.
  • Player Experience Goals: The goal of this game is to cause the player to be afraid of the NPC's and be on the edge of their sets.
  • Genre: This game is a fun twist on the traditional zombie survival genre casting the player as the zombie.
  • Game play: You are a lone zombie on desert road. Defend yourself against the guns of the survivors and feast upon the flesh of the innocents.
    The goal of this game is to kill the most innocent civilians with out being killed by the surviving fighters, blue clothed men. Each kill gives you 10 points, but each hit lowers your health by 10. Use the arrow keys to move around and collide with the civilians, orange guys, to attack them.
    In future developments the game could be expanded to include the turning of humans in to zombies, allowing the player to control a herd of zombies.
  • Prototype goal: The goal of this prototype is to explore the use of a dynamic system that spawns enemies at random that attack the player and raise the difficulty.
  • Features: The random spawning of enemies and raises the difficulty of the game as time goes on. This feature creates a unique game that changes depending on how long you play.
    This game also implements 8-bit particle effects that create a stylized but realistic game world.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a zombie alone on a desert road, fighters and innocent civilians sometimes get lost and that is when you feed. You must avoid the strong armed fighters and feed on the weak civilians in order to survive. Be warned though as this road is not abandoned and its hard to survive a car crash even as a zombie.
  • Story: You are a lone zombie seek only one thing, to satisfy your hunger with the flesh of humans. Seek after the innocent women and children and eat their organs to continue on in your undead life.
  • Target Audience: The target audience of this game is fans of zombie related media as well as fans of the survival horror genre.
  • Play time: This game is a short one level prototype that takes anywhere between a minute to five minutes to fully explore.
  • Strategies: The player should try to avoid the fighters in order to preserve their health. They should also be careful when crossing the street as a car hit will kill the player.
